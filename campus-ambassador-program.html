Campus Ambassador Program

Learn and Earn

All with loads of fun!

The Campus Ambassador program at Coding Blocks is aimed at identifying energetic, passionate, brilliant, like minded College Superheroes (and this term Superhero includes all the Supermen & Wonderwomen) who have the zeal to take initiative and make a mark.

How to identify a Coding Blocks Superhero? Well, they are the people who are typically great coders and very active members of Computer societies, clubs, events and activities. In addition, they are stars of their community and people look upto them since they usually lead by example. In short, Leadership flows in their veins and they are the visionaries of tomorrow.

If you feel you have the qualities to be a Coding Blocks Superhero, we invite you to join the clan and be a part of the community. We assure you of a great learning experience, awesome perks and take home rewards, a super confident you, a shining resume with recommendations & certificate and a place in the Coding Blocks family. Here are the details, in case you have already made up your mind to be a part of us.

Roles and Responsibilities of Coding Blocks Superheroes

  1. Be the official Super Hero alias Ambassador and branding representative of Coding Blocks in your college
  2. Create programming contests on HackerBlocks (our online coding practice tool)
  3. Promote our courses in your college and get students to sign up & subscribe (including YouTube)
  4. Publicise by displaying posters on notice boards, sharing e-posters on WhatsApp, Facebook etc, circulating emails, etc.
  5. Maintain relevant databases and submit monthly reports
  6. Conduct Coding Blocks workshop every 3 months and programming events / online hackathon every 45 days in your campus
  7. Organize counselling sessions and meet-ups in college canteen/campus with Mentors & Alumni
  8. Represent us in the placement cells of your college
  9. Come up with innovative ideas on how Coding Blocks can assist your college and associate us with any technical fest or sessions that your college conducts
  10. Put Coding Blocks “Books” in college library
  11. Introduce BOSS event of Coding Blocks in Tech Talks
  12. Get students to attend difficult subject doubt session ‘Webinars’ – organised by Mentors of Coding Blocks
  13. Associate Coding Blocks with various college societies and fest department
  14. Any other marketing initiative will be rewarded as well

What You Earn & Learn

  1. Superhero kit with host of swags like T-shirt, Bag, Cap, Diary, Pen drive, Stickers and so on
  2. Earn great Cash rewards on organising workshops in your college
  3. Student referral rewards
  4. Work Experience Certificate to all eligible candidates on successful completion of six months program
  5. LinkedIn recommendation to all eligible candidates after completion of program
  6. Meet targets, get our online courses for free
  7. Free book worth Rs 1200 if targets are met
  8. Free passes for workshops at Coding Blocks
  9. Opportunity to interact and network with industry experts and professionals through various events
  10. Opportunity to become a part of us (PPO)
  11. Huge cash incentives for specific target achievements
  12. Additional cash incentives for blogs writing for Coding Blocks
  13. An exposure of working with a start-up, with some of the best brains in the industry
  14. Campus ambassadors are preferred for Paid internships and Teaching Assistants at Coding Blocks
  15. Can sit in Coding Blocks placement drives even if non-CB student (haven’t enrolled for a course)
  16. Acclamation of efforts through Superheros of the Month, Quarter and Year entitling you to Cask rewards, Mobile phone and laptop respectively.
